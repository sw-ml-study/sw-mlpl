Added svg(pts, "scatter3d") renderer for static 3-D point clouds: accepts [N,3] or [N,4], orthographic projection at fixed azimuth=30/elevation=20, one <circle> per point, small labeled axis gizmos at a fixed canvas position, optional sorted legend for 4-column input. Deterministic output (snapshot-tested on unit-cube-corners). 9 tests cover shape, legend presence, axis labels, snapshot stability, and error paths. Contract at contracts/viz-contract/scatter3d.md.
