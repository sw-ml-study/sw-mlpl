Implemented parser for literals and array literals. Parser struct with parse() entry producing Vec<Expr>. Handles int/float/negative literals, identifiers, flat and nested array literals, empty arrays, parenthesized expressions, multi-statement with newline/semicolon separation. Added UnexpectedToken and UnclosedDelimiter error variants. 14 integration tests. Kept to 7 functions in parser.rs by inlining parse_atom into parse_expr.
