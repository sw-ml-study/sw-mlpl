Added line and bar svg diagram types. Reorganized svg.rs into svg/ directory with submodules (helpers, scatter, line, bar, mod) to satisfy function-count limit. render_line accepts vectors (x=0..N-1, loss curves) or Nx2 matrices (xy pairs) and emits <polyline>. render_bar takes vectors and emits one <rect> per element with baseline at zero, supporting negative values. Dispatcher matches scatter|line|bar. 10 new tests passing.
