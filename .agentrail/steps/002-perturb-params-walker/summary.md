Added perturb_params(m, family, sigma, seed) builtin that walks a model's params, filters by one of four families (all_layers | attention_only | mlp_only | embed_and_head), and adds sigma * randn(seed+i, shape) to each matching param in place. Final-projection-head detection is structural (last top-level Linear child of the outermost Chain) so mlp_only / embed_and_head don't conflate MLP linears with the vocab projection. Deterministic per (family, sigma, seed); returns scalar 0.0 unit. 12 tests cover touch-set invariants, head exclusion, determinism, different-seed divergence, sigma=0 no-op, and three error paths.
