# The MLPL Philosophy

MLPL is built on four core pillars:

### 1. Array-First Thinking
Inspired by Kenneth Iverson's APL, we believe that the notation you use should actively help you think. By treating arrays as first-class citizens, we eliminate the need for manual loops and boilerplate data manipulation.

### 2. Rust-Powered Safety
The MLPL runtime is written in Rust, ensuring that your computations are not only fast but memory-safe. We bring the discipline of the Rust ecosystem to the dynamic world of machine learning experimentation.

### 3. Visual Feedback
If you can't see your data, you don't understand it. MLPL integrates visualization into its core syntax, allowing you to generate SVG representations of your tensors directly from your code.

### 4. Agentic Interoperability
MLPL is designed for the modern world of AI-assisted development. Its concise syntax and clear structure make it an ideal language for LLMs to generate and for human developers to verify.
