# Introduction

Welcome to the definitive guide to MLPL. This book is designed to take you from your first "Hello, Array" to building and visualizing complex machine learning models.

MLPL was born from a simple observation: machine learning is fundamentally about transformations of data shapes. By combining the concise power of APL-style array programming with the safety and performance of Rust, MLPL provides a unique environment for researchers, engineers, and curious minds to explore the world of tensors.
