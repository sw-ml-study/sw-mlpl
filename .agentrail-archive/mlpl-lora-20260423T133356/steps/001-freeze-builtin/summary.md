Added freeze(m) / unfreeze(m) builtins plus env.frozen_params. Frozen params still receive gradients via the tape (chain rule unchanged) but adam and momentum_sgd continue past them at the parameter-update stage -- the weight stays bit-identical while the rest of the model trains. Overlay, not replacement: frozen names are still in env.params. Argument shape matches clone_model/perturb_params (bare ident or Value::Model expression). 9 tests cover the overlay invariant, adam/sgd parity under freeze, unfreeze round-trip, partial-freeze isolation, idempotency, and error paths.
