Shipped tsne(X, perplexity, iters, seed) builtin decomposed into four single-responsibility modules (tsne_builtin entry/orchestrator, tsne_validate arg validation, tsne_affinities P matrix in 3 phases, tsne_gradient per-step gradient descent in 3 phases). Every phase is a pure function; the orchestrator reads top-to-bottom. 9 tests including structure-preservation on 3 Gaussian blobs, deterministic under identical seeds, every error path. Also shipped docs/sw-checklist-patterns.md (~200 lines) cataloguing the decomposition patterns with real examples from this codebase, plus a companion feedback_sw_checklist_patterns.md memory.
